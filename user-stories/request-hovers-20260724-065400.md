## Requirements / Acceptance Criteria

This page shows exactly three user avatar images in a row. Each image has
a hidden caption overlay containing a name ("user1"/"user2"/"user3") and a
"View profile" link, which only becomes visible when the mouse hovers over
that specific image. Moving the mouse away hides the caption again.

Acceptance Criteria:
- AC1: On page load, exactly 3 avatar images are present, and no caption
  overlay or "View profile" link is visible for any of them.
- AC2: Hovering over the first image reveals its caption ("user1") and a
  "View profile" link; the other two images' captions remain hidden.
- AC3: Hovering over the second image reveals its caption ("user2") and a
  "View profile" link; the other two images' captions remain hidden.
- AC4: Hovering over the third image reveals its caption ("user3") and a
  "View profile" link; the other two images' captions remain hidden.
- AC5: Moving the mouse away from a hovered image (e.g. hovering a
  different image, or moving off all images) hides that image's caption
  again.
- AC6: Clicking the "View profile" link while hovering navigates to that
  user's profile page (URL pattern /users/<n>), without needing to submit
  a form or reload manually first.
- AC7: The page never unexpectedly navigates or reloads just from hovering
  (only an explicit click on "View profile" navigates).

No login or test credentials are required; this page has no authentication.
